## About Seller's Compliance

Seller's Compliance was founded to solve a specific, recurring problem in the Southern California real estate market: homeowners reaching the final stages of a transaction only to discover unresolved compliance issues that delay or jeopardize the sale.

We provide compliance inspections and installation services that help homeowners, sellers, and real estate professionals get ahead of these issues — before they become costly surprises.

We operate throughout **Los Angeles County** and **Orange County**, two of the most active real estate markets in the country.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Real estate transactions in California require sellers to meet specific compliance standards. These include:

- Working smoke and carbon monoxide detectors
- Water heaters that are properly strapped to prevent tipping in an earthquake
- Properly installed overflow pipes on water heaters
- Low-flow plumbing fixtures that meet California water conservation requirements
- Seismic gas shutoff valves where required

When these items are out of compliance, escrow can be delayed, buyers can get cold feet, and sellers can face unexpected costs at the worst possible moment.

Seller's Compliance removes that risk.
