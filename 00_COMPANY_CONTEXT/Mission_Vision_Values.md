# Mission, Vision, and Core Values

---

## Mission

To help homeowners, sellers, real estate professionals, and property managers identify and resolve residential compliance issues before they create delays or complications during a real estate transaction.

---

## Vision

To be the most trusted compliance inspection and installation service in Southern California — the company that real estate professionals recommend without hesitation and that homeowners rely on when it matters most.

---

## Core Values

### 1. Reliability
We show up when we say we will. We do what we say we'll do. In a transaction-driven industry, reliability is everything.

### 2. Clarity
We explain compliance issues in plain language. Our clients should always understand what needs to be done and why — not feel confused or taken advantage of.

### 3. Speed
Real estate moves fast. We match the pace of our clients' transactions and don't create unnecessary delays.

### 4. Expertise
We specialize. Compliance is not a side service for us — it's everything we do. That focus makes us better at it.

### 5. Trust
We earn the trust of real estate professionals and homeowners by being consistent, honest, and easy to work with. Our reputation is built one transaction at a time.

---

## Brand Promise

*We take compliance off your plate so your transaction can move forward.*
