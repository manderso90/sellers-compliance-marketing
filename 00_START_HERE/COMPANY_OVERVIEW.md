## What We Do

Seller's Compliance performs residential compliance inspections and related installation services throughout **Los Angeles County** and **Orange County**, California.

We help homeowners, sellers, real estate professionals, escrow officers, and property managers resolve compliance issues before they delay or complicate real estate transactions.

---

## Core Services

- Compliance inspections
- Smoke detector evaluations and installations
- Carbon monoxide detector evaluations and installations
- Water heater strapping
- Water heater overflow pipe installation
- Low-flow plumbing fixture compliance
- Seismic gas shutoff valve evaluations and installations
