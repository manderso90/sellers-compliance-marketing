# Service: Compliance Inspection

## What It Is

A thorough evaluation of a residential property's compliance status across all items required by California law for real estate transactions.

## What's Evaluated

- Smoke detector presence, placement, and function
- Carbon monoxide detector presence, placement, and function
- Water heater strapping
- Water heater overflow pipe
- Low-flow plumbing fixtures (toilets, showerheads, faucets)
- Seismic gas shutoff valve (where applicable)

## Who Needs It

- Home sellers preparing to list or close escrow
- Property managers maintaining portfolio compliance
- Homeowners seeking peace of mind or preparing to rent

## Key Differentiator

We specialize in compliance inspections — this is not a side service. We know exactly what's required for LA and OC properties and can identify issues that a general home inspector might miss or mischaracterize.

## Documentation

We provide documentation that can be used to satisfy compliance disclosure requirements in a real estate transaction.

## Related Services

After inspection, Seller's Compliance can perform all required installations in the same visit or as a follow-up — smoke detectors, water heater strapping, low-flow fixtures, etc.
