# Service: Seismic Gas Shutoff Valve Evaluations and Installations

## What It Is

Evaluation and installation of automatic seismic gas shutoff valves that stop gas flow when they detect earthquake activity above a set threshold.

## What It Does

In the event of a significant earthquake, gas lines can rupture — creating fire and explosion hazards. A seismic shutoff valve automatically closes the gas supply before that can happen.

## California Requirements

- Requirements vary by jurisdiction
- Some cities in Los Angeles County and Orange County require seismic shutoff valves
- Others do not
- A compliance inspection determines whether one is required for the specific property

## What We Do

- Determine whether a seismic shutoff valve is required for the property's jurisdiction
- Evaluate whether one is present and functional if required
- Install seismic shutoff valves where required

## Common Issues Found

- Required by local ordinance but not installed
- Older or malfunctioning valve that no longer meets current standards
