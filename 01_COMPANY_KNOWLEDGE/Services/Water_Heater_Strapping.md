# Service: Water Heater Strapping

## What It Is

Installation of seismic anchor straps on residential water heaters to prevent them from tipping or falling during an earthquake.

## California Requirements

- Water heaters must be secured with **two straps** — one in the upper third of the unit, one in the lower third
- A single strap does not meet code
- Required disclosure and compliance item in all California residential real estate transactions

## What We Do

- Evaluate existing strapping for compliance
- Install dual-strap systems to current California code

## Why It Matters

An unstrapped water heater can tip in an earthquake and rupture the gas line — creating a serious fire and explosion hazard. This is one of the most important seismic safety measures a homeowner can take, and one of the most commonly overlooked.

## Common Issues Found

- No strapping present
- Single strap only
- Straps improperly attached to drywall instead of structural framing
- Corroded or deteriorated strapping

## Marketing Notes
