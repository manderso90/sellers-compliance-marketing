## Core Services

### 1. Compliance Inspections

A thorough evaluation of a residential property's compliance status across the items required by California law for real estate transactions.

**What's evaluated:**
- Smoke detector presence, placement, and function
- Carbon monoxide detector presence, placement, and function
- Water heater strapping
- Water heater overflow pipe
- Low-flow plumbing fixtures
- Seismic gas shutoff valve (where applicable)

**Who needs it:**
- Home sellers preparing to list or close
- Property managers maintaining portfolio compliance
- Homeowners seeking peace of mind

---

### 2. Smoke Detector Evaluations and Installations

California requires working smoke detectors in every bedroom, outside sleeping areas, and on every level of the home.

**What we do:**
- Evaluate existing smoke detectors for compliance
- Install new detectors where required
- Replace battery-only units with hardwired or 10-year sealed battery units where required

---

### 3. Carbon Monoxide Detector Evaluations and Installations

California requires CO detectors in homes with attached garages, gas appliances, or fireplaces.

**What we do:**
- Evaluate existing CO detectors for compliance
- Install new detectors where required

---

### 4. Water Heater Strapping

California law requires water heaters to be strapped to prevent them from falling or tipping during an earthquake. This is a mandatory disclosure and compliance item in real estate transactions.

**What we do:**
- Evaluate existing strapping
- Install dual-strap systems to code

---

### 5. Overflow Pipe Installation

Water heaters require a properly installed temperature-pressure relief (TPR) valve overflow pipe that directs water safely in the event of a pressure release.

**What we do:**
- Evaluate existing overflow pipe installation
- Install or correct overflow pipes to comply with local code

---

### 6. Low-Flow Plumbing Fixture Compliance

California requires low-flow toilets, showerheads, and faucets as part of the real estate disclosure and compliance process.

**What we do:**
- Evaluate existing fixtures for compliance
- Install compliant low-flow fixtures where needed

---

### 7. Seismic Gas Shutoff Valve Evaluations and Installations

Some California jurisdictions require automatic seismic gas shutoff valves that stop gas flow in the event of an earthquake.

**What we do:**
- Evaluate whether a seismic shutoff valve is required and present
- Install seismic shutoff valves where required

---
