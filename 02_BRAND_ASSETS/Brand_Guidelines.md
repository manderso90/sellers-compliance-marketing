## Voice and Tone

### Voice (consistent across all content)
- **Expert:** We know compliance. We speak with authority.
- **Clear:** No jargon. Plain language always.
- **Trustworthy:** We don't oversell. We inform.
- **Local:** We are a Southern California business. We know this market.

### Tone (varies by context)

| Context | Tone |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn (B2B) | Professional, informative, peer-to-peer |
| Facebook (B2C) | Friendly, approachable, helpful |
| Email | Direct and respectful |
| Website | Confident and clear |

---

## Writing Standards

- Use "Seller's Compliance" (with apostrophe) on first reference. "We" or "our team" on subsequent references in the same piece.
- Capitalize service names when used formally (e.g., "Compliance Inspection").
- Avoid passive voice where possible.
- Keep sentences short. Real estate clients are busy.
- Never invent or estimate compliance rules. Refer to documented requirements.

---

## What We Are Not

- We are not a general contractor.
- We are not a home inspector (we do compliance inspections, not general home inspections).
- We are not a plumber or electrician (though our services touch those systems).

These distinctions matter for accurate marketing.
