# LinkedIn Strategy

LinkedIn is the primary B2B channel for Seller's Compliance. The goal is to build relationships with real estate agents, brokers, and property managers in LA and OC.

---

## Objective

Position Seller's Compliance as the go-to compliance resource for real estate professionals in Southern California.

---

## Target Audience on LinkedIn

- Real estate agents and brokers (LA and OC)
- Real estate team leaders and brokerage owners
- Property managers and property management companies
- Transaction coordinators
- Real estate investors

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. Education
Teach real estate professionals about compliance requirements. Make them look smart in front of their clients.

Examples:
- "What every seller needs to know about smoke detector compliance in California"
- "The 5 compliance items that delay the most escrows in LA"
- "Why water heater strapping matters (and what happens when it doesn't exist)"

### 2. Trust and Social Proof
Share evidence of reliability and expertise.

Examples:
- Client testimonials (from agents who refer us)
- "We completed X inspections this month"
- Before/after photos of compliance installations

### 3. Market and Industry Insight
Demonstrate knowledge of the real estate market.

Examples:
- Commentary on local real estate trends as they relate to compliance
- Updates when compliance regulations change

### 4. Behind the Scenes
Humanize the brand. Show the work.

Examples:
- A day in the field
- The team at work
- Photos from jobs (with permission)

---

## Posting Cadence

Target: **3–5 posts per week**

| Day | Content Type |
|---|---|
| Monday | Educational post |
| Wednesday | Social proof / testimonial |
| Friday | Industry insight or behind-the-scenes |

Adjust based on performance data.

---

## Post Format Guidelines

- Keep posts under 1,300 characters for full visibility without "see more"
- Use line breaks to improve readability
- End with a question or call to action
- Use 3–5 hashtags (not more)
- Avoid generic stock photos — use real job photos when possible

---

## Engagement Strategy

- Respond to all comments within 24 hours
- Engage with posts from target accounts (agents, brokerages in LA/OC)
- Connect with agents after referrals or completed jobs
- Join and participate in real estate LinkedIn groups

---

## Key Metrics to Track

- Follower growth
- Post impressions and reach
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Connection requests from target audience
- Direct messages and leads from LinkedIn
