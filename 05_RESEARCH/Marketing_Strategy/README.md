# Marketing Strategy

Strategic research, analysis, and planning documents.

## What Goes Here

- Audience research findings
- Channel strategy decisions and rationale
- Campaign planning documents
- Marketing experiment results and learnings
- Anything strategic that doesn't belong in a project folder

## Distinction from Projects

Projects (`06_PROJECTS/`) are active, time-bound initiatives.
Strategy documents here are reference material — they inform projects but aren't projects themselves.
