# Command: /competitor-review

**Usage:** `/competitor-review [company name or website]`

**Skill:** `07_AI_INSTRUCTIONS/Skills/Analyze_Competitor.md`

---

## What It Does

Researches a competitor and produces a structured profile covering services, messaging, online presence, strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for Seller's Compliance.

## Examples

```
/competitor-review [Competitor Company Name]
/competitor-review [website URL]
/competitor-review focus on their social media strategy
```

## Output

A completed competitor profile saved to `05_RESEARCH/Competitor_Research/Competitor_[Name].md`, plus a bullet summary of the most actionable findings.
