# Command: /content-plan

**Usage:** `/content-plan [month]`

**Skill:** `07_AI_INSTRUCTIONS/Skills/Build_Content_Calendar.md`

---

## What It Does

Builds a complete monthly content calendar for both LinkedIn and Facebook. Checks existing published content to avoid repetition, aligns with current priorities, and outputs a ready-to-use planning document.

## Examples

```
/content-plan July 2026
/content-plan August — focus on realtor education content this month
/content-plan next month, we have Pete's water heater photos to use
```

## Output

A completed monthly content calendar saved to `03_SOCIAL_MEDIA/Content_Calendar/YYYY-MM_Content_Calendar.md`.
