# Command: /linkedin-post

**Usage:** `/linkedin-post [topic or description]`

**Skill:** `07_AI_INSTRUCTIONS/Skills/Create_LinkedIn_Post.md`

---

## What It Does

Drafts a LinkedIn post for Seller's Compliance. Follows all brand voice, platform, and audience guidelines automatically.

## Examples

```
/linkedin-post about water heater strapping for real estate agents
/linkedin-post compliance checklist for agents
/linkedin-post using Pete's photo of a completed installation
/linkedin-post about smoke detector expiration dates
```

## Output

A complete LinkedIn post draft including hook, body, CTA, and hashtags — ready for review. Saved to `03_SOCIAL_MEDIA/LinkedIn/Drafts/`.
