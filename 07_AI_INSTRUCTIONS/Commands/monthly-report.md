# Command: /monthly-report

**Usage:** `/monthly-report [month]`

**Skill:** `07_AI_INSTRUCTIONS/Skills/Monthly_Marketing_Report.md`

---

## What It Does

Generates a monthly marketing summary: posts published, performance highlights, wins, gaps, and next-month priorities. Pulls from published post archives and active project notes.

## Examples

```
/monthly-report June 2026
/monthly-report last month
```

## Output

A completed monthly report saved to `08_MEETINGS_AND_NOTES/YYYY-MM_Monthly_Report.md`.
