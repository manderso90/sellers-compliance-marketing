# Command: /photo-analysis

**Usage:** `/photo-analysis [describe photos or share them]`

**Skill:** `07_AI_INSTRUCTIONS/Skills/Turn_Photos_Into_Content.md`

---

## What It Does

Takes job photos from Pete and generates social media post concepts and captions for LinkedIn, Facebook, or both.

## Examples

```
/photo-analysis before and after water heater strapping job
/photo-analysis Pete's photos from today's smoke detector install in Burbank
/photo-analysis photo of unstrapped water heater — LinkedIn post for agents
```

## Output

1–2 draft posts per platform with captions, ready for review. Saved to appropriate `Drafts/` folders.
