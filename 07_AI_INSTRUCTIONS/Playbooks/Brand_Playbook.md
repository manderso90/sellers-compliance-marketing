# Brand Playbook

Strategic knowledge for maintaining and growing the Seller's Compliance brand. A living document — update as brand standards evolve.

---

## What the Brand Stands For

Seller's Compliance is the company that makes compliance simple. We remove a stressful, confusing obstacle from real estate transactions and replace it with a smooth, professional process.

The brand promise is: **We take compliance off your plate.**

---

## Brand Personality

If Seller's Compliance were a person, they would be:

- A knowledgeable professional who explains things clearly
- Someone who shows up on time and does what they say
- Not a salesperson — more like a trusted advisor
- Calm and competent, especially when clients are stressed
- Local — they know LA and OC, not just compliance in general

---

## Voice in Practice

| Situation | Voice |
|---|---|
| Explaining a compliance requirement | Clear, factual, no jargon |
| Talking to an agent | Peer-to-peer, professional, efficient |
| Talking to a stressed seller | Reassuring, straightforward, human |
| Sharing a job photo | Confident but not boastful |
| Responding to a negative review (if ever) | Calm, professional, solution-focused |

---

## Things We Never Do

- We don't use fear to generate business ("your home could be a fire hazard!!")
- We don't make legal claims or give legal advice
- We don't compare ourselves negatively to competitors by name
- We don't use compliance jargon without explaining it
- We don't post generic, could-be-anyone content

---

## Brand Consistency Checklist

Before publishing any content, ask:
- [ ] Is the tone professional and helpful — not salesy?
- [ ] Is it accurate? (No invented compliance rules)
- [ ] Is it clear to someone who doesn't know compliance terminology?
- [ ] Does it serve the reader first?
- [ ] Does it reflect a local, trusted, expert company?
