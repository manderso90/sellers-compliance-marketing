# Facebook Playbook

Strategic knowledge for Seller's Compliance's Facebook presence. A living document — update as the team learns what works.

---

## Purpose of Facebook for Seller's Compliance

Facebook is our B2C channel. The primary goal is to reach homeowners and sellers in LA and OC who need compliance help — either directly or through people in their network.

Unlike LinkedIn (where we build professional relationships), Facebook is where we build awareness and trust with the general public. A homeowner who doesn't know us today might recall our name when their agent mentions compliance.

**The Facebook goal:** Be recognizable and trustworthy to homeowners in LA and OC before they need us.

---

## What Works on Facebook

**Simple, clear educational posts**
Homeowners don't know compliance terminology. Posts that explain things plainly — without assuming knowledge — perform well. "Here's what a water heater strap does and why California requires one" outperforms "Water heater strapping compliance tips."

**Before/after photos from Pete**
Real job photos build more trust than any written post. A photo of an unstrapped water heater next to one properly strapped, with a simple caption, is among the highest-performing content types.

**Reassurance**
Many sellers first encounter compliance during escrow when they're already stressed. Posts that say "this is manageable, here's what it means" tend to get saved and shared.

**Local references**
Mentioning specific cities or neighborhoods in LA and OC increases relevance. People respond more to content that feels like it's for them specifically.

---

## What Doesn't Work on Facebook

- Industry jargon without explanation
- Hard-sell posts
- Generic stock photography
- Complex multi-point posts without visual breaks
- Posts that assume the reader already knows what compliance is

---

## The Photo Workflow

Pete's job photos are among our most valuable content assets. When Pete completes a job:

1. Pete shares photos (before and after where possible)
2. Save originals to `02_BRAND_ASSETS/Photos/`
3. Use `/photo-analysis` to generate post drafts from the photos
4. Schedule the best versions into the content calendar

Every job is a content opportunity.

---

## Engagement Strategy

- Respond to all comments and messages promptly
- People who comment or message are potential clients — treat them accordingly
- Encourage satisfied clients to leave reviews (Google, Facebook)
