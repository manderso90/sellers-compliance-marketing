# LinkedIn Playbook

This playbook captures how Seller's Compliance approaches LinkedIn marketing. It is a living document — update it as the team learns what works.

---

## Purpose of LinkedIn for Seller's Compliance

LinkedIn is our B2B channel. The primary goal is not to generate direct sales — it's to build relationships with real estate professionals who will refer their clients to us.

Real estate agents are our best referral partners. If an agent trusts us and knows we'll take care of their clients, they will send us business repeatedly.

**The LinkedIn goal:** Be the compliance company that LA/OC real estate agents think of first.

---

## Content That Works on LinkedIn

Based on the real estate professional audience, these content types tend to perform well:

**Educational posts:** "Here's something your clients often don't know about compliance..."
These make agents look knowledgeable when they share or reference them with clients. Agents love content that helps them serve clients better.

**Practical tips:** Short, actionable checklists and facts
Easy to engage with and share. Spreads the brand to their networks.

**Transaction stories:** "Here's how we helped a client close on time after a compliance issue was discovered late..."
Social proof with narrative. Agents relate to these situations.

**Industry commentary:** Brief takes on real estate market conditions as they relate to compliance
Positions us as industry insiders, not just a vendor.

---

## Content That Doesn't Belong on LinkedIn

- Overly promotional posts ("Call us today!")
- Hard-sell messaging
- Generic stock photo content
- Irrelevant personal content

---

## Writing a LinkedIn Post

1. **Lead with the insight or hook** — not with the company name. Get to the point.
2. **Use short lines and line breaks** — LinkedIn is not a wall of text medium.
3. **Be specific** — "California requires dual water heater straps" is better than "compliance matters."
4. **End with engagement** — A question, a call to share, or a call to reach out.
5. **3–5 hashtags only** — #SellersCompliance #LosAngelesRealEstate #ComplianceInspection etc.

---

## Relationship Building on LinkedIn

Posting is only half the strategy. The other half is engagement:

- Connect with agents after referrals or completed jobs
- Comment meaningfully on posts from target accounts
- Respond to all comments on our posts within 24 hours
- DM prospects who engage with our content
- Join real estate LinkedIn groups and participate regularly
