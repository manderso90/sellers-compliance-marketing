# Realtor Outreach Playbook

Strategic knowledge for building relationships with real estate agents in LA and OC. A living document — update as the team learns what works.

---

## Why Realtors Are the Priority

A single real estate agent who trusts Seller's Compliance can refer dozens of clients per year. Agents handle multiple transactions simultaneously and need reliable compliance vendors they can refer without hesitation.

Building realtor relationships is the highest-leverage growth channel for Seller's Compliance.

---

## What Realtors Need From a Compliance Vendor

1. **Reliability** — Show up, do the work, communicate clearly. Don't make them look bad.
2. **Speed** — Escrow doesn't wait. Fast turnaround is a competitive advantage.
3. **Documentation** — They need paperwork for their files. Make it easy.
4. **Clear communication** — They should always know the status. Never leave them guessing.
5. **Expertise** — They want someone who knows compliance cold so they don't have to.

---

## Outreach Channels

### LinkedIn (Primary)
- Connect with agents after completing a job for their client
- Engage meaningfully with their posts — not just likes, actual comments
- Share content that makes them look knowledgeable when they share it
- DM agents who engage with our content

### Direct Outreach
- Introduce the company to local brokerages
- Offer to speak at team meetings or trainings on compliance topics
- Provide "compliance cheat sheets" they can give clients

### Referral Follow-Through
- Every time an agent refers a client: make the experience exceptional
- Send a thank-you after the job is complete
- Make it easy for them to refer again (they shouldn't have to remember our number)

---

## Content for Realtors

Realtors are most likely to engage with content that:
- Makes them look smart to their clients
- Saves them time (answers questions they'd otherwise have to answer)
- Helps their transactions close smoothly
- Is specific and actionable (not vague "compliance tips")

Examples that work:
- "5 compliance items that delay the most escrows in LA County"
- "What your clients need to know about smoke detector requirements before listing"
- "Why water heater strapping is always in the inspection report (and how to get ahead of it)"

---

## The Agent Relationship Loop

```
Agent hears about SC (LinkedIn / referral / outreach)
    ↓
Agent refers a client
    ↓
SC delivers exceptional service + documentation
    ↓
Agent follows up (thank you, easy to re-refer)
    ↓
Agent refers again + tells colleagues
```

Every interaction either strengthens or weakens the loop.
