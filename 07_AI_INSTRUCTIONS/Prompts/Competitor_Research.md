# Prompt: Competitor Research

Use this prompt for structured competitor investigation. For a full profile, use the `/competitor-review` command instead.

---

## Prompt Template

```
Research [COMPETITOR NAME] as a competitor to Seller's Compliance.

Website: [URL if known]
What I want to understand: [e.g., their content strategy / their pricing / their messaging to realtors]

Please investigate:
1. Their services and how they describe them
2. Their online presence (website, Google rating, social media)
3. What their customers say (reviews, complaints, praise)
4. How they position themselves vs. Seller's Compliance
5. Where they appear strong and where they have gaps

Use the template in `05_RESEARCH/Competitor_Research/Competitor_Research_Template.md`.
Save the completed profile to `05_RESEARCH/Competitor_Research/Competitor_[Name].md`.

Note the date of research at the top — competitive data goes stale.
```

---

## When to Use

- Researching a specific competitor mentioned by a client or agent
- Quarterly competitive landscape check
- Before launching a new campaign (understand what's already in the market)
