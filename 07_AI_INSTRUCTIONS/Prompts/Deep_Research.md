# Prompt: Deep Research

Use this prompt when you need Claude to do thorough research on a topic — not a quick summary, but a complete investigation.

---

## Prompt Template

```
I need a thorough research report on [TOPIC].

Context:
- Company: Seller's Compliance (residential compliance inspections, LA and OC)
- Purpose: [Why this research matters — e.g., "to understand how to reach property managers on LinkedIn"]
- Audience for this report: [Who will use the findings — e.g., "Arynn, to inform content strategy"]

Please cover:
1. [Key question 1]
2. [Key question 2]
3. [Key question 3]

Format the output with:
- An executive summary (3–5 bullet points)
- Detailed findings organized by question
- Recommendations for Seller's Compliance specifically
- Sources or confidence level for each claim

Save findings to: [file path]
```

---

## When to Use

- Deep competitor analysis
- Understanding a new marketing channel
- Researching a compliance topic before creating educational content
- Investigating a new audience segment (e.g., escrow officers, investors)

---

## Example Uses

- Deep research on how real estate agents use LinkedIn and what content performs best in that community
- Deep research on California water heater strapping requirements and history
- Deep research on property manager compliance needs in LA County
