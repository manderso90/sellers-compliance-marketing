# Prompt: SEO Research

Use this prompt when researching keywords, search intent, or content opportunities for organic search.

---

## Prompt Template

```
Help me research SEO opportunities for Seller's Compliance.

Company: Residential compliance inspections and installations in LA County and Orange County, CA.
Goal: [e.g., understand what homeowners search for when they need compliance help / find content topics that target real estate agents via search]

Please identify:
1. High-value keyword clusters for our services
2. Questions people are likely searching that we could answer
3. Local search opportunities (LA-specific, OC-specific, city-level)
4. Content gaps — topics competitors rank for that we don't address

Cross-reference with existing research in `05_RESEARCH/SEO_Research/SEO_Keyword_Research.md` and avoid duplicating what's already documented.

Add any new keywords or content opportunities to the SEO keyword file.
```

---

## When to Use

- Expanding the SEO keyword list
- Identifying blog or FAQ topics with search demand
- Evaluating whether a content idea has search value
- Quarterly SEO strategy review
