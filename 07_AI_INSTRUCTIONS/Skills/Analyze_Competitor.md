# Skill: Analyze Competitor

**Triggered by:** `/competitor-review [company name or URL]`

---

## Purpose

Research a competitor and produce a structured profile for the Seller's Compliance research library.

---

## Inputs

- **Company name** and/or website URL
- **Any specific angle to investigate** (pricing, content strategy, audience, etc.)

---

## Process

1. **Review existing research.** Check `05_RESEARCH/Competitor_Research/` — has this competitor already been profiled?

2. **Gather public information.** Research:
   - Website (services, messaging, positioning, pricing if visible)
   - Google Business Profile (rating, review count, review themes)
   - LinkedIn presence (follower count, posting frequency, content types)
   - Facebook presence (follower count, posting frequency, engagement)
   - Yelp or other review platforms

3. **Analyze strengths and weaknesses.** Based on findings:
   - Where do they appear strong?
   - Where are there gaps, complaints, or weaknesses?
   - What content topics do they own vs. ignore?
   - What does their audience seem to respond to?

4. **Identify opportunities for Seller's Compliance.** Where can we differentiate?

5. **Complete the competitor profile** using the template: `05_RESEARCH/Competitor_Research/Competitor_Research_Template.md`

6. **Save the completed profile** as `05_RESEARCH/Competitor_Research/Competitor_[CompanyName].md`

---

## Output

- Completed competitor profile (saved to `05_RESEARCH/Competitor_Research/`)
- 3–5 bullet summary of the most actionable findings
- Recommended content or positioning opportunities for Seller's Compliance

---

## Important

Do not make claims about a competitor that cannot be verified from public sources. Note the date of research — competitive landscapes change.
