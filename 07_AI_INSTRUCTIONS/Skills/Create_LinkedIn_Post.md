# Skill: Create LinkedIn Post

**Triggered by:** `/linkedin-post` command

---

## Purpose

Draft a LinkedIn post for Seller's Compliance using the brand voice and content strategy defined in this repository.

---

## Inputs

When executing this skill, provide one of the following:

- **Topic:** A topic, compliance tip, or subject to write about
- **Pillar:** The content pillar (Education / Social Proof / Industry Insight / Behind the Scenes)
- **Angle:** A specific angle or hook to use

If no input is given, choose an educational topic from `04_CONTENT_LIBRARY/Compliance_Tips.md`.

---

## Process

1. **Review the audience.** This post is for LinkedIn. Primary audience: real estate agents and brokers in LA and OC. Write for them.

2. **Check existing content.** Before writing from scratch, check `04_CONTENT_LIBRARY/` for relevant facts, tips, or copy to build from.

3. **Draft the post.**

   Structure:
   - **Hook (line 1):** A single compelling line that makes someone stop scrolling.
   - **Body:** 3–6 short paragraphs or bullet points. Clear, specific, useful.
   - **CTA:** A question, call to engage, or call to reach out.
   - **Hashtags:** 3–5 relevant hashtags. Always include #SellersCompliance.

4. **Apply brand voice check:**
   - Is it clear and jargon-free?
   - Does it sound like an expert, not a salesperson?
   - Is it specific to LA/OC where relevant?
   - Would a real estate agent find this useful or shareable?

5. **Output the draft** ready for review. Do not publish; save drafts to `03_SOCIAL_MEDIA/LinkedIn/Drafts/`.

---

## Example Output Format

```
[Hook line that stops the scroll]

[Body — short lines, punchy, clear]

[CTA]

#SellersCompliance #LosAngelesRealEstate #[relevant hashtag]
```

---

## Notes

- Do not invent compliance rules. Only reference documented requirements from `01_COMPANY_KNOWLEDGE/` or `04_CONTENT_LIBRARY/`.
- Keep posts under 1,300 characters for full visibility without "see more."
- Images should be suggested but not required (AI cannot source real photos).
