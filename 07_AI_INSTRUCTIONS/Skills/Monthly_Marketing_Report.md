# Skill: Monthly Marketing Report

**Triggered by:** `/monthly-report [month]`

---

## Purpose

Generate a monthly marketing summary for Seller's Compliance that captures what was done, what performed well, and what to prioritize next month.

---

## Inputs

- **Month being reported**
- **Content published** (or review `03_SOCIAL_MEDIA/LinkedIn/Published/` and `03_SOCIAL_MEDIA/Facebook/Published/`)
- **Any performance data available** (follower counts, engagement, leads generated)
- **Projects completed or advanced**

---

## Process

1. **Gather published content.** Review `Published/` folders for the month. Count posts by platform and pillar.

2. **Review active projects.** Check `06_PROJECTS/Active_Projects.md` — what moved forward this month?

3. **Compile performance data.** Use whatever data is available (manual from platform dashboards). Note gaps where data isn't available yet.

4. **Identify wins.** What worked? What got the most engagement? What generated leads or inquiries?

5. **Identify gaps.** What didn't get done? What content pillars were underrepresented?

6. **Recommend next month's priorities.** Based on the above, what should be focused on?

7. **Output the report** as `08_MEETINGS_AND_NOTES/YYYY-MM_Monthly_Report.md`

---

## Output Format

```
## [Month Year] Marketing Report

### Summary
[2–3 sentence overview]

### Content Published
- LinkedIn: X posts
- Facebook: X posts
- Breakdown by pillar: ...

### Performance Highlights
[Key metrics or observations]

### Wins
- ...

### Gaps / Missed
- ...

### Priorities for Next Month
1. ...
2. ...
3. ...
```

---

## Notes

- If platform analytics aren't available yet, note what data is missing and how to collect it going forward.
- The report is for internal use — it doesn't need to be polished, it needs to be accurate and useful.
- Over time, monthly reports become a valuable record of what was tried and what worked.
