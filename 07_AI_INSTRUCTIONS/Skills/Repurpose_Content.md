# Skill: Repurpose Content

**Triggered by:** `/repurpose-content [source content]`

---

## Purpose

Take an existing piece of content and adapt it for a different platform, format, or audience — without starting from scratch.

---

## Inputs

- **Source content** (paste the original post, article, or tip)
- **Source platform** (where it originally appeared)
- **Target platform or format** (where the repurposed version will go)
- **Target audience** (if different from the original)

---

## Common Repurposing Paths

| From | To |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn post (agent-focused) | Facebook post (homeowner-focused) |
| Facebook post | LinkedIn post |
| Long educational post | 3-part series of shorter posts |
| Compliance tip | FAQ entry for the content library |
| Multiple tips | Checklist post |
| Customer story | Testimonial post |
| Blog or web content | Social post series |

---

## Process

1. **Read the source content carefully.** What is the core insight or story?

2. **Identify what changes for the new context:**
   - Different platform? Adjust tone, length, and format.
   - Different audience? Reframe the angle (agent lens vs. homeowner lens).
   - Different format? Restructure (long → short, narrative → list, etc.)

3. **Rewrite — don't just copy.** Repurposed content should feel native to its new platform, not like a cross-post.

4. **Check accuracy.** Any compliance facts in the original must remain accurate in the repurposed version.

5. **Save the new version** to the appropriate `Drafts/` folder.

---

## Output

- Repurposed draft ready for review
- Note of any changes made and why
- Flag if anything in the original content should be updated or corrected before reusing
