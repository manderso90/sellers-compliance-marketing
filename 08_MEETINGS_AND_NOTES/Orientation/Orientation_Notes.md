## Workspace Setup — June 2026

### What Was Built

The Seller's Compliance Marketing Workspace was initialized with a full folder structure and foundational content:

- `00_START_HERE/` — Quick-reference launchpad (company overview, priorities, team directory)
- `00_COMPANY_CONTEXT/` — Mission, values, company story, customer profiles (ICPs)
- `01_COMPANY_KNOWLEDGE/` — Services (individual files per service), FAQs, pricing placeholder
- `02_BRAND_ASSETS/` — Brand guidelines and color palette framework (assets to be added)
- `03_SOCIAL_MEDIA/` — LinkedIn and Facebook strategies, content calendar template, published post archives
- `04_CONTENT_LIBRARY/` — Compliance tips library, educational content
- `05_RESEARCH/` — Competitor research template, SEO keyword framework, industry research, social media ideas
- `06_PROJECTS/` — Active project tracking
- `07_AI_INSTRUCTIONS/` — CLAUDE.md, context files, assistant guides, playbooks, skills, commands
- `08_MEETINGS_AND_NOTES/` — This folder
- `09_ARCHIVE/` — Archive with year subfolders

### Key Decisions Made

- **LinkedIn is the primary B2B channel** — target: real estate agents, brokers, property managers, escrow officers
- **Facebook is the primary B2C channel** — target: homeowners and sellers
- **Content philosophy: teach first, sell second** — educational content is the primary vehicle for trust-building
- **Arynn** is the primary operator of this workspace
- **Pete** (field/operations) is the source of job photos and field knowledge

### Tools in Use

- VS Code
- GitHub (repository: `manderso90/sellers-compliance-marketing`)
