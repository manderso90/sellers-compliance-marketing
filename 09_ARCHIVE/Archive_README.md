# Archive

This folder contains completed projects, historical content, and retired materials.

---

## Purpose

Nothing should be permanently deleted unless absolutely necessary. Completed work belongs here.

Archiving preserves institutional knowledge — a campaign that ran 18 months ago might have lessons that inform future work.

---

## How to Archive

When a project or piece of content is no longer active:

1. Move it to this folder (or a subfolder organized by year or project name).
2. Add a brief summary at the top of the document noting:
   - When it was active
   - What the outcome was
   - Any lessons learned

---

## Archive Structure (evolves over time)

```
09_ARCHIVE/
  2026/
    [Project Name]/
  2027/
    [Project Name]/
```

---

## What Belongs Here

- Completed marketing campaigns
- Retired social media content series
- Old versions of strategy documents
- Past content calendars
- Concluded projects from `06_PROJECTS/`
