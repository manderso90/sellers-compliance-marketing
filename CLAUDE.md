## Who We Are

**Seller's Compliance** provides residential compliance inspections and related installation services in **Los Angeles County and Orange County, California**.

We help homeowners, sellers, real estate agents, and property managers resolve compliance issues before they delay or complicate real estate transactions.

---

## Core Services

- Compliance inspections
- Smoke detector evaluations and installations
- Carbon monoxide detector evaluations and installations
- Water heater strapping
- Overflow pipe installation
- Low-flow plumbing fixture compliance
- Seismic gas shutoff valve evaluations and installations

---

## Who We Serve (Primary Audiences)

1. **Home Sellers** — Need to pass compliance before close of escrow. Time-sensitive, often stressed.
2. **Real Estate Agents** — Refer clients to us. Trust and reliability are paramount. We make them look good.
3. **Property Managers** — Manage multiple properties; need reliable, efficient compliance services.
4. **Homeowners** — May need compliance for refinancing, renting, or general peace of mind.

---

## Brand Voice

- **Clear and confident** — We are the experts. We speak plainly, not in jargon.
- **Helpful and educational** — We want clients to understand what compliance means and why it matters.
- **Professional but approachable** — Not stuffy. We work with regular homeowners, not just industry professionals.
- **Local and trusted** — We know LA and OC. We are a local business, not a national chain.

## How to Work in This Repository

### Before Creating New Content

1. Check `04_CONTENT_LIBRARY` for reusable content.
2. Check `07_AI_INSTRUCTIONS/Playbooks` for the relevant playbook.
3. Check `06_PROJECTS` for the active project context.
4. Review `00_COMPANY_CONTEXT` if you need brand or audience grounding.

### Content Creation Standards

- All content should reflect the brand voice above.
- Educational content should be accurate. Do not invent compliance rules.
- Social media posts should be concise, clear, and actionable.
- Always think about the reader's situation: are they stressed about a sale? Unfamiliar with compliance? A professional who needs quick facts?

### File Naming Conventions

- Use `Title_Case_With_Underscores.md` for documents.
- Prefix dated files with `YYYY-MM-DD_`.
- Use folder structure as context. A post in `03_SOCIAL_MEDIA/LinkedIn/Drafts/` is a draft LinkedIn post.

### What Not to Do

- Do not invent company history, pricing, or services that aren't documented here.
- Do not create content that contradicts existing brand guidelines.
- Do not delete or overwrite files without understanding their purpose.
- When in doubt, create a new file rather than editing an existing one.
