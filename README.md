# Seller's Compliance Marketing Workspace

## Purpose

Welcome to the Seller's Compliance Marketing Workspace.

This repository serves as the central marketing operating system for Seller's Compliance. It is designed to organize company knowledge, brand assets, marketing projects, social media content, research, and AI-assisted workflows into a single source of truth.

The goal is to create a durable, scalable marketing system that can be used by current and future team members while preserving institutional knowledge and supporting efficient collaboration with AI tools such as Claude Code.

---

# About Seller's Compliance

Seller's Compliance provides residential compliance inspections and related installation services throughout Los Angeles County and Orange County.

Our mission is to help homeowners, sellers, real estate professionals, and property managers identify and resolve compliance-related issues before they create delays or complications during a real estate transaction.

Core services include:

* Compliance inspections
* Smoke detector evaluations and installations
* Carbon monoxide detector evaluations and installations
* Water heater strapping
* Overflow pipe installation
* Low-flow plumbing fixture compliance
* Seismic gas shutoff valve evaluations and installations
